Our Educational Programs

Tailored educational stages designed to nurture students' intellectual, social, and emotional growth.

Ages 5-10

Primary Education

Building a strong foundation with an emphasis on literacy, numeracy, and curiosity through engaging hands-on activities.

  • Play-based learning
  • Foundational skills
  • Creative arts

Middle School

Guiding students through critical transitional years, focusing on critical thinking, collaborative projects, and self-discovery.

  • Advanced sciences
  • Technology integration
  • Athletics programs

High School

Preparing young adults for higher education and future careers with college-preparatory coursework and leadership opportunities.

  • Advanced Placement (AP)
  • Career counseling
  • Leadership council
